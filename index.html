Welcome to Vist

With Vist, you can make a beautiful list of videos with an optional homepage script. All you need to do is fill in the blanks in the "videos" code and repeat for all videos.

Why should I use it?

With Vist you can put your company's videos in an easy to access list without directing people to your YouTube channel. It is great to show your friends your favourite videos. No matter who you are, you can use Vist. It is fully customizable so you can also replace things like the background image easily.
